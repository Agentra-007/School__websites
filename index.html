Our Story

Rooted in Patna.
Reaching for Excellence.

Founded in 1985 by visionary educationist Dr. Ramesh Prasad Sinha, XYZ Academy began as a small English-medium school with 120 students and a single building in Boring Road, Patna. Today, we stand as Bihar's most trusted CBSE-affiliated institution with over 4,200 students, 180 faculty members, and a sprawling 8-acre campus.

Our mission is simple: "To nurture every child's unique brilliance through academic excellence, character formation, and a lifelong love of learning."

Curriculum

A Programme Built for Every Stage

Three thoughtfully designed wings, each calibrated to the developmental needs of your child — from play-based learning to board examination mastery.

Classes I – V · Ages 6–11

Foundation for Life-Long Learning

Our Primary Wing focuses on building a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional skills through activity-based, joyful learning. We follow the NEP 2020 framework integrated with CBSE guidelines.

English, Hindi & Sanskrit (Languages)
Mathematics with Abacus & Vedic Maths enrichment
Environmental Studies (EVS) with outdoor fieldwork
Art, Music, and Physical Education (every day)
Computer Science & Coding (from Class III)
Value Education & Yoga
